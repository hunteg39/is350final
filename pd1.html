Title:A Christmas Carol
Author:Charles Dickens
ISBN:9781580495790

"A Christmas Carol" is a time honored book close to the hearts of many. Published in 1843 by Charles Dickens, it has been the subject of many plays and movies. The story is about a man named Ebenezer Scrooge with a distain for the holidays and a love of money who is visited by three spirits and learns the meaning of Christmas.
